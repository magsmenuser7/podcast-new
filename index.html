The podcast aims to highlight and inspire young and innovative entrepreneurs from Andhra Pradesh, showcasing their journeys, challenges, and successes to motivate others. By sharing these stories, we hope to create a benchmark of excellence and creativity that will encourage the next generation of entrepreneurs to rise and shine.

At InTalks, we believe every story has the power to inspire, educate, and provoke thought. Born out of a passion for meaningful conversations, we aim to bridge the gap between ideas and audiences by creating a platform where diverse voices are heard.
